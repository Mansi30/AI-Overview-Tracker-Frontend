🔒 Privacy Policy

AI Overview Tracker | Last updated: November 11, 2025

📌 Quick Summary

Your data is YOURS. Everything is stored in your personal Firebase database. We cannot access, view, or share your data. Period.

1. Overview

AI Overview Tracker is a Chrome extension and analytics dashboard built for researchers, SEO professionals, and content creators to analyze Google's AI Overview citations. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data with complete transparency.

Developer: Aditya Dubey / SimPPL

2. What Data We Collect

Our extension collects the following data when you use Google Search:

  • 🔍Search Queries: The text you enter in Google Search (to detect AI Overview appearances)
  • 🔗Citation Data: URLs, domains, and titles of sources cited in AI Overviews
  • 🖱️Click Events: Which citations you click, timestamps, and time-to-click metrics
  • 🏷️Query Metadata: Automatically categorized topics (technology, health, finance, etc.) and query intent
  • 📊Session Data: Session IDs for grouping related searches
  • 🤖AI Response Text: The full text of AI Overview responses for analysis

3. How Data is Stored

Your data is stored in two locations, both under your complete control:

  1. Chrome Local Storage: Temporarily cached in your browser (chrome.storage.local) for offline access and performance
  2. Firebase Firestore: Synced to your personal Firebase database for persistence and cross-device access

🔐 Critical Privacy Point

All data resides in YOUR Firebase project. We (the developers) do not have access to your database. Your Firebase credentials are stored locally in your extension only.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Display analytics and insights in your personal dashboard
  • Calculate citation statistics (CTR, engagement metrics, domain rankings)
  • Show search trends and patterns over time
  • Categorize queries by topic and intent
  • Track user engagement with citations
  • Generate visualizations (charts, graphs, timelines)

That's it. No other purposes. No hidden uses.

5. What We DON'T Do

We absolutely do NOT:

  • ❌ Share your data with any third parties
  • ❌ Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising purposes
  • ❌ Track you across other websites
  • ❌ Access your personal Firebase database
  • ❌ Collect data when the extension is disabled
  • ❌ Store data on our servers (we don't have any!)
  • ❌ Analyze your data in aggregate

6. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • 👁️View: Access all tracked data in the analytics dashboard
  • 🗑️Delete: Clear all data from extension settings or Firebase Console
  • ⚙️Configure: Set data retention period (7, 15, 30, or 90 days)
  • 📥Export: Download your data from Firebase Console anytime
  • 🛑Stop Tracking: Disable or uninstall the extension to stop data collection immediately
  • 🔒Control Access: Configure Firebase security rules as you see fit

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by multiple layers of security:

  • Firebase Security: Enterprise-grade Google Cloud infrastructure
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transfers use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Firestore Security Rules: Configurable access controls (managed by you)
  • Local Storage: Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • No Central Database: No single point of failure or data breach risk

8. Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage, hosting, and authentication (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Search: Extension runs only on Google Search pages (no data shared with Google)

We do NOT integrate with: Advertising networks, analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics), tracking services, or any other third-party data collectors.

9. Data Retention

Data retention is controlled entirely by you:

  • Default: 90 days
  • Configurable Options: 7, 15, 30, or 90 days
  • Automatic Cleanup: Old events are automatically deleted based on your settings
  • Manual Deletion: Delete all data anytime from extension settings
  • Uninstall: Data remains in your Firebase (you control it), but new data stops being collected

10. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through this extension, please contact us immediately and we will provide guidance on data deletion.

11. International Users

This extension can be used globally. Your data is stored in Firebase's cloud infrastructure, which may be located in various regions. Firebase complies with international data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or extension features. When we make changes:

  • The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised
  • Significant changes will be announced in extension update notes
  • Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance

13. Your Consent

By installing and using AI Overview Tracker, you consent to:

  • This privacy policy and our data practices
  • Collection of data as described above
  • Storage of data in your personal Firebase database

You can withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the extension.

14. Legal Compliance
